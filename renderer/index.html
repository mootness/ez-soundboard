🎧 Discord & Audio Routing Setup

Discord records your microphone, not your speakers. To send soundboard clips into Discord you need a virtual audio cable — software that makes one app's output appear as another app's microphone input.

Step 1 — Install VB-Cable (free, one-time)

Download from vb-audio.com/Cable and run the installer as Administrator. Restart your PC if prompted. This adds two virtual devices: CABLE Input and CABLE Output.

Step 2 — Configure EZ Soundboard
🔊 Output CABLE Input (VB-Audio Virtual Cable)
🎧 Monitor Your headphones / speakers (so you still hear clips)
Step 3 — Configure Discord

Settings → Voice & Video → Input Device → CABLE Output (VB-Audio Virtual Cable)

⚠ VB-Cable is not included — it must be installed separately. Without it, the Output and Monitor dropdowns above will only show your physical devices and Discord routing will not work.
